Callao, Perú

Official Flag of Perú

Callao, Perú, is a vibrant port city founded in 1537, making it one of the oldest cities in the country. It is home to the largest seaport in Peru and the main airport, Jorge Chávez International Airport. Additionally, Callao features the Palomino Islands, where visitors can observe a large population of sea lions and various seabirds in their natural habitat.
