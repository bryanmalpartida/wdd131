Peruvian Skate History

First Peruvian in Street League

In 2019, Angelo Caro became the first Peruvian skateboarder to compete in the prestigious Street League Skateboarding (SLS) contest. His participation marked a historic moment for Peruvian skateboarding, showcasing the talent and dedication of local skaters on an international stage. Angelo is also known for representing Perú in the 2020 Tokyo Olympics, where skateboarding made its Olympic debut.

First Visit by US Skateboarders

In 2015 and 2019 , a group of professional skateboarders from the United States sponsored by Nike SB and DC visited Perú for the first time. Their visit inspired local skaters and helped grow the skateboarding community in the country.
